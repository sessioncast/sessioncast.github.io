Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through Stripe.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The Free plan lets you try all core features. You can upgrade to a paid plan when you need more sessions or agents.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

You won't be able to create new sessions or agents beyond your plan limits. Upgrade your plan to get more capacity.
